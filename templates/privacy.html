Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and a hashed version of your password. When you use the app, we store the expense records you create — including amounts, categories, dates, and any descriptions you provide.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively to provide the Spendly service — displaying your expenses, generating summaries, and personalizing your experience. We do not use your financial data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the app on your behalf.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on our servers. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed before being saved. We take reasonable technical precautions to protect your information from unauthorized access, but no system is completely immune to risk.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. We may use trusted infrastructure providers (such as hosting services) to operate the app, but they are bound by confidentiality obligations and are not permitted to use your data independently.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us. We are committed to addressing your concerns and maintaining your trust as a user of Spendly.
